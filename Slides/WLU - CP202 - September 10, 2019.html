For Startups:

Social Platform Connectivity

Social platform that connects investors with startups in the KW region based on search keywords (sector/industry, market-size, desired capital, geographic location) via 3 major platforms (Web, Android, iOS)

Pool of Investors

Transforms traditional approaches to seed funding for startups by allowing regular people to participate in a pool of investors and raise capital

For Investors:

Eliminates Asymmetry Problem

Fully encrypted chat function that allows investors to communicate directly with startups of interest. Allows investors to conduct screening prior to due diligence.

Trust and Ease of Access

The platform contains information on credentials of the management team, their vision, total funding secured, and future goals. Investors will also be notified of startups that match investors' criteria of interest.
